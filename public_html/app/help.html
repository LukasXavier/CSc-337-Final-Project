Rules

  • The player's turn is indicated by a yellow star above their hand
  • To draw a card click on the middle of the play area
  • If it is your turn the only cards that can be played are cards that match the color or value of the last played card except for the "$" and "?" cards
  • The special cards "$" and "?" can be used as a wild card and are not restricted by value or color
  • After playing a "$" or "?" card you will be given a pop-up menu to select a color that will be applied to the card
  • Special Cards:
    • $ - Lets the player select its color and the next player will have to draw 4 cards
    • ? - Lets the player select its color
    • # - Skips the next player's turn
    • + - The next player will have to draw 2 cards
    • % - Reverses the turn order
